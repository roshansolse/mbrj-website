Frequently Asked Questions

  • How Many Years You Are Working In This Industry?

  • Why should I hire a professional photographer?

    Hiring a professional photographer offers numerous advantages. Here are a few key reasons why you should consider hiring a professional:


    • Expertise and Skill: Professional photographers have extensive knowledge and skills in capturing high-quality photographs. They understand composition, lighting, and how to bring out the best in their subjects. Their experience allows them to consistently deliver exceptional results.
    • Creativity and Vision:Professional photographers have a unique artistic vision. They possess the ability to conceptualize and create captivating images that tell a story and evoke emotions. They can transform ordinary moments into extraordinary works of art.
    • Specialized Equipment: Professional photographers use high-quality cameras, lenses, and other equipment to ensure the best possible outcome. They are well-versed in utilizing the latest technology and techniques to capture stunning images that may not be achievable with consumer-grade equipment.
    • Attention to Detail: Professional photographers pay meticulous attention to every detail, from composition to post-processing. They have an eye for capturing the perfect shot, ensuring that no significant moments or nuances are missed.
    • Professionalism and Reliability: When you hire a professional photographer, you can rely on their professionalism and dedication to their craft. They will arrive on time, be prepared for the shoot, and deliver the promised results within the agreed-upon timeframe.
    • Personalized Service: Professional photographers work closely with their clients to understand their vision, preferences, and specific requirements. They tailor their approach to ensure that your unique style and personality shine through in the photographs.
    • Preserving Memories: Professional photographers excel at capturing precious moments and preserving them for a lifetime. Whether it's a wedding, family portrait, or special event, they ensure that the memories are beautifully documented, allowing you to cherish them for years to come.

    Remember, photography is an art form, and professional photographers bring their expertise, creativity, and technical skills to create images that go beyond what a typical snapshot can achieve. By hiring a professional, you invest in capturing moments that will be cherished forever.

  • What types of photography do you do?

    As a professional photographer, I specialize in various types of photography to cater to a wide range of needs and preferences. Some of the photography genres I excel in include:


    • Wedding Photography: Capturing the beautiful moments, emotions, and details of weddings, from the ceremony to the reception.
    • Portrait Photography:Creating stunning portraits that highlight the unique personality and essence of individuals or groups.
    • Family Photography: Documenting precious moments and relationships within families, creating lasting memories to be cherished.
    • Event Photography: Covering a diverse range of events, such as corporate gatherings, conferences, parties, and other special occasions.
    • Commercial/Product Photography: Showcasing products or services for advertising and promotional purposes, ensuring high-quality images that capture attention.
    • Landscape/Nature Photography: Capturing the beauty of landscapes, nature, and the environment, showcasing the awe-inspiring scenes of the world around us.
    • Fashion Photography: Creating visually striking images for fashion brands, designers, or magazines, showcasing clothing, accessories, and models.

    These are just a few examples, and I am versatile in adapting to various photography styles and genres. I strive to provide customized photography services that align with the unique needs and desires of my clients.

  • Do You Have Your Own Studio or Office?

    Yes, we have a dedicated studio/office where we conduct photography sessions, client consultations, and handle administrative tasks. Our studio is equipped with professional lighting, backdrops, and other necessary equipment to ensure high-quality photography.

    It provides a comfortable and controlled environment for capturing exceptional images. Additionally, our office serves as a central hub for client meetings, discussing project details, showcasing our portfolio, and handling any necessary paperwork.

  • Do You Provide Costumes & Props?

    Yes, as part of our photography services, we provide a wide range of costumes and props to enhance the creative and thematic aspects of your photoshoot. Whether you're looking for specific attire, accessories, or props to complement your vision or theme, we have a diverse selection to choose from.

    Pricing is based on Quotation you choose. Our goal is to help bring your ideas to life and create visually captivating images that reflect your unique style and concept. We can discuss your preferences and requirements during the planning process to ensure that we have the appropriate costumes and props available for your photoshoot.

  • Will I Get Raw Photos & How?

    Yes, we understand the value of giving our clients the flexibility and control over their photography experience. Therefore, we do offer the option of providing raw data files, upon request. Raw data refers to the unprocessed, original image files captured by the camera, containing all the information and details captured in the photograph.

    While we take great care in curating and enhancing the final images to deliver the best results, we recognize that some clients may prefer to have access to the raw files for their own editing or archiving purposes.

    Please let us know if you are interested in obtaining the raw data files, and we can discuss the details further to ensure we meet your specific requirements.

  • How much time is required for editing?

    The time required for editing varies depending on the scope and complexity of the project, as well as the specific editing requests. After the photoshoot, we carefully review and select the best shots to edit and enhance.

    For smaller projects, such as portrait sessions or events, the editing process typically takes a few days to a week. This allows us to meticulously retouch the images, adjust color balance, optimize exposure, and make any necessary artistic enhancements while maintaining a high level of quality.

    For larger projects or weddings, where a larger number of images are involved, the editing process may take a bit longer. In such cases, we aim to deliver the edited photos within two to four weeks. This ensures that we have ample time to give each image the attention it deserves to meet our professional standards.

    We prioritize delivering a final product that surpasses your expectations while maintaining a reasonable turnaround time. During our initial consultation, we can discuss your specific timeline requirements, and we will do our best to accommodate them.

    Please note that our primary focus is on delivering high-quality, visually stunning images rather than rushing through the editing process. This allows us to ensure that every image meets our standards of excellence.
